Frequently Asked Questions

Why isn't it completely free?

The free tier is genuinely free forever and better than most paid alternatives. Premium features like unlimited AI search, auto-context detection, and iCloud sync help support ongoing development while keeping the core experience free for everyone.

How is this cheaper than competitors?

We're open source and privacy-first with no expensive cloud infrastructure. This means lower costs ($7.99/year vs Paste's $14.99), better privacy, and superior features.

What happens when my free AI searches run out?

You get 10 semantic AI searches per day on the free plan. If you hit the limit, keyword search still works (unlimited), OCR still works, and all other features continue. AI searches reset at midnight.

Is there a free trial of Premium?

Yes! Try all Premium features free for 14 days with no credit card required. After the trial, the free tier activates automatically. Upgrade anytime to Premium ($7.99/year).
