Refund Policy

Last updated: January 4, 2026

1. Overview

We want you to be completely satisfied with ClipboardManager Pro. This refund policy explains our refund process for paid licenses.

2. Free Tier

ClipboardManager offers a generous free tier with no payment required. We encourage you to thoroughly test the free version before purchasing a Pro license to ensure the app meets your needs.

Free tier includes:

  • 250 clipboard items
  • 30-day retention period
  • Keyword search
  • OCR text extraction
  • All core features

3. 30-Day Money-Back Guarantee

We offer a 30-day money-back guarantee on all Pro license purchases (Lifetime, Annual, and Monthly).

If you are not satisfied with ClipboardManager Pro for any reason, you may request a full refund within 30 days of your purchase date.

4. Refund Eligibility

You are eligible for a full refund if:

  • The refund request is made within 30 days of purchase
  • You have a valid proof of purchase (order confirmation email)
  • The license has not been shared with others
  • You have not violated our Terms of Service

5. How to Request a Refund

To request a refund, please contact Paddle support directly:

  1. Visit Paddle Support
  2. Provide your order number (found in your purchase confirmation email)
  3. Explain the reason for your refund request (optional but appreciated)
  4. Paddle will process your refund within 5-7 business days

Alternatively, you can open an issue on our GitHub repository and we will assist you with the refund process.

6. Refund Processing Time

Refunds are processed by Paddle, our payment processor:

  • Approval time: 1-2 business days
  • Refund to original payment method: 5-7 business days
  • Bank processing: May take additional 3-5 days depending on your bank

You will receive an email confirmation when your refund has been processed.

7. Subscription Cancellations

Annual and Monthly Subscriptions:

  • You can cancel your subscription at any time
  • Cancellation prevents future charges
  • You retain Pro access until the end of your current billing period
  • No partial refunds for unused time within the current period (except within 30-day guarantee window)
  • To cancel: Log in to your Paddle account or contact Paddle support

8. Lifetime License Policy

Lifetime licenses ($29.99 launch special):

  • Eligible for full refund within 30 days of purchase
  • After 30 days, no refunds are provided (as this is a one-time perpetual license)
  • Continue to receive updates and support for the lifetime of the product

9. Non-Refundable Situations

Refunds will not be provided in the following cases:

  • Refund requests made after 30 days from purchase date
  • Licenses shared with or resold to others
  • Violations of our Terms of Service
  • Attempting to reverse engineer or bypass license validation
  • Chargebacks initiated without contacting us first (results in permanent ban)

10. Technical Issues

If you experience technical issues with ClipboardManager Pro:

  • Please open an issue on our GitHub repository first
  • We will work with you to resolve the issue
  • If the issue cannot be resolved, a full refund will be provided regardless of the 30-day window

11. Launch Special (First 500 Users)

The $29.99 lifetime license launch special is subject to the same 30-day money-back guarantee. However, due to the limited nature of this offer (first 500 users only), once refunded, you will not be able to re-purchase at the launch special price.

12. Downgrades

You can downgrade from Pro to the free tier at any time:

  • Deactivate your license in the app settings
  • Cancel your subscription to prevent future charges
  • Your clipboard history will be preserved (subject to free tier limits)
  • Items exceeding the 250-item limit will not be deleted, but new items won't be added until you're under the limit

13. Changes to This Policy

We reserve the right to modify this refund policy at any time. Changes will be posted on this page with an updated "Last updated" date. Your continued use of ClipboardManager after changes constitutes acceptance of the modified policy.

14. Contact Information

For refund requests or questions about this policy:

  • Paddle Support: paddle.com/support
  • GitHub Issues: github.com/dcrivac/ClipboardManager/issues

We stand behind our product and are committed to your satisfaction. If ClipboardManager Pro doesn't meet your expectations, we'll gladly provide a refund within 30 days of purchase.
