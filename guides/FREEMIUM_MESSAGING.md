## FAQ Section

### "Why isn't it completely free?"

**Answer:**
> Clipso's basic version is 100% free forever‚Äîbetter than most paid alternatives. Premium features like unlimited AI search, automatic context detection, and iCloud sync help support ongoing development while keeping the core experience free for everyone.

### "How is this cheaper than competitors?"

**Answer:**
> We're open source and privacy-first, which means:
> - No expensive cloud infrastructure (everything runs locally)
> - No investor pressure to maximize revenue
> - Lower prices while delivering superior features
>
> **Result:** $7.99/year vs Paste's $14.99‚Äîyou save $7 annually.

### "What happens when my free AI searches run out?"

**Answer:**
> You get 10 semantic AI searches per day on the free plan. If you hit the limit:
> - Keyword search still works (unlimited)
> - OCR still works (unlimited)
> - All other features still work
> - AI searches reset at midnight
>
> For unlimited AI, upgrade to Premium for $7.99/year.

### "Is there a free trial of Premium?"

**Answer:**
> Yes! Download and try all Premium features free for 14 days. No credit card required. After the trial:
> - Free tier activates automatically (no downgrade needed)
> - Upgrade anytime to Premium ($7.99/year)

### "Can I pay once instead of yearly?"

**Answer:**
> Yes! We offer a lifetime license for $29.99 (one-time payment). This includes:
> - All Premium features forever
> - All future updates free
> - No recurring charges

### "Where does my payment go?"

**Answer:**
> Your premium subscription supports:
> - Ongoing development (new features, bug fixes)
> - Infrastructure (website, documentation)
> - Open source maintenance
> - Keeping the free tier generous
>
> We're committed to keeping Clipso affordable and privacy-focused.
