## Brand Voice Guidelines

**Tone**: Confident but not arrogant, technical but accessible, privacy-focused

**Do**:
- Be direct about advantages
- Use technical specifics when relevant
- Emphasize privacy and open source
- Show, don't just tell (demos, examples)

**Don't**:
- Make unverifiable claims
- Dismiss competitors unfairly
- Overhype features
- Use marketing jargon without substance

**Example Good**: "Semantic search using Apple's NLEmbedding finds items by meaning in <50ms"
**Example Bad**: "Revolutionary next-gen AI-powered super-intelligent clipboard"
